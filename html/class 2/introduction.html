indian tourism

Indian tourism thrives on the country's diverse landscapes, from Himalayan peaks to Goan beaches, and its incredibly rich cultural heritage, evident in ancient monuments like the Taj Mahal and vibrant festivals. It acts as a significant driver for the Indian economy, generating income and employment, while also playing a vital role in preserving national heritage and promoting cross-cultural understanding.
